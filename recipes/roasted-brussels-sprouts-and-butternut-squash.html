Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Butternut Squash

Description

Simple roasted veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 2 cups cubed butternut squash
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the Brussels sprouts and squash with the olive oil until thoroughly coated. Mix in the garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper. Spread the vegetables in a single layer onto a foil-lined baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until golden and crispy, about 25 minutes.
